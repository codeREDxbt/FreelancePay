# Pitch Deck Outline

**1. Title Slide**
- FreelancePay: Trustless Milestone Escrow & Cross-Border Payouts

**2. The Problem**
- Trust is broken in global freelancing.
- Freelancers suffer from delayed payments and unpaid invoices.
- Clients fear paying upfront for incomplete or subpar work.

**3. The Solution**
- Programmable milestone escrow on Stellar.
- Clients lock funds upfront; freelancers get paid instantly upon milestone approval.

**4. Why Now?**
- The remote work economy is growing rapidly.
- Cross-border payments remain slow and expensive.
- Programmable stablecoins (USDC on Stellar) are finally ready for mainstream apps.

**5. Product Walkthrough**
- (Include screenshots: Dashboard, Contract Creation, Approval Flow)
- Simple onboarding, clear milestone tracking, zero friction.

**6. Architecture**
- Frontend: Next.js + React
- Smart Contracts: Soroban (Rust)
- Backend & Data: Firebase + PostHog Analytics

**7. Why Stellar?**
- Low fees (fractions of a cent).
- Sub-5-second finality.
- Built-in anchor network for fiat on/off ramps.

**8. Traction & Proof**
- 50+ unique testnet users.
- X amount of contracts created and milestones funded.
- 4.4/5 average user rating from our in-app feedback widget.

**9. Feedback-Driven Iteration**
- How we listened to users:
  - Built a guided "Sample Contract" flow to ease onboarding.
  - Added Activity Feeds for better transparency.
  - Created URL invites for viral growth.

**10. Growth Strategy**
- Target Stellar builder communities first.
- Expand to web3-native design and dev agencies.
- Viral loop: every contract brings 1 new user (the counterparty).

**11. Roadmap**
- Q3: Mainnet launch & fiat ramps integration.
- Q4: Multi-asset support (EURC, XLM).
- Q1: Decentralized dispute resolution.

**12. Closing**
- Join the beta. Let's build the future of work.
