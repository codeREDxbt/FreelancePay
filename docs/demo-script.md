# Demo Script (4-6 minutes)

## 1. Intro (20–30 sec)
"Freelancers lose billions each year to unpaid invoices and delayed payments, while clients fear paying upfront for work that may never arrive. 
FreelancePay solves this by combining programmable milestone escrow with Stellar's fast, low-fee cross-border payouts."

## 2. Onboarding (45 sec)
"Let's see how easy it is to start. I simply click 'Connect Wallet' and log in with Freighter. No heavy KYC required up front.
To demonstrate, I'll use our new 'Try Sample Contract' feature, which instantly scaffolds a real-world scenario."

## 3. Client flow (1.5 min)
"As a client, I'm creating a 'Q4 Website Development' contract. 
I can see my predefined milestones here. The pre-submit review shows me exactly how much USDC will be locked in the smart contract.
I click execute, and in seconds, the funds are locked on the Stellar testnet."

## 4. Freelancer flow (1 min)
"I can easily invite my freelancer via a URL. 
Switching to the freelancer view, they see the contract is funded. 
They complete the first milestone, attach a Figma link, and submit it for review."

## 5. Client approval (45 sec)
"Back as the client, I review the work. Everything looks good, so I hit 'Approve'.
The smart contract instantly releases the first milestone payment directly to the freelancer's wallet. Fast, cheap, and trustless."

## 6. Proof & analytics (45 sec)
"If we look at the contract's Activity Feed, we see a permanent, chronological record of every state change, backed by real Stellar transactions.
On the backend, our PostHog dashboard confirms active usage, and our in-app feedback widget captures user sentiment immediately after they fund or approve milestones."

## 7. Closing (20 sec)
"With 50+ testnet users already transacting, we're ready to expand beyond testnet. FreelancePay: bringing programmable trust to the global gig economy."
